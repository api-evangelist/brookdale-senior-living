The article addresses five common misconceptions about dementia perpetuated by media and pop culture, noting that people with dementia can maintain independence in early stages, retain decision-making abilities, and preserve their sense of self as the disease progresses. It explains that behavioral changes typically reflect communication needs rather than aggression, and that lifestyle modifications can help protect cognitive function.
