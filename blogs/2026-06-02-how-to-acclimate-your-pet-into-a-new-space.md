Moving presents challenges not just for humans but also for pets, who may experience stress from unfamiliar environments, sounds, and smells. The article recommends introducing pets gradually to their new home rather than giving them immediate access to the entire space, and notes that familiar scents from toys and bedding help pets adjust. Dogs benefit from neighborhood walks to acclimate and burn energy, while cats may need days or weeks confined to one or two rooms before feeling comfortable.
